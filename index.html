The Craft

Made by Hand.
Made to Last.

Every Promesse piece is made entirely by hand in our Kigali atelier. We work only with sustainably sourced Rwandan hardwoods — selected plank by plank for grain, density, and character. No two pieces are identical.

Our craftsmen bring decades of woodworking tradition to every cut, shape, and finish. The result is furniture and kitchenware that grows more beautiful with time.
