The Process

How We Work

01

Brief

Share your vision — dimensions, wood preferences, timeline, and budget — via our contact form or in person at our studio.

02

Design

Our team develops material samples, technical drawings, and a production plan. We revise until you're fully satisfied.

03

Craft

Our master artisans hand-make your piece from sustainably sourced hardwood in our Kigali studio — no shortcuts, no mass-production.

04

Deliver

White-glove delivery and installation across Kigali and all of Rwanda. International shipping available on request.
